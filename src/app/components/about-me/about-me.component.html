About me

Hi, I am Kai, a full-stack software developer from Ducherow, Germany. Since 2023 I have been working at 11880 in a team that develops internal software for telephony, agents, team leaders, and service processes.

My work focuses on business-critical applications: telephony systems connected to Multiconnect and Salesforce, tools for secretarial services, queue and opening-hour management, audio settings for service numbers, and qualification or level settings for agents.

I enjoy building software that is used in real daily workflows. Clean structures, reliable interfaces, and understandable user interfaces are important to me. Today I mainly work with React, Java, PostgreSQL, REST APIs, Keycloak, Wiki.js, WildFly, and Linux systems. I also follow AI development closely and use tools like OpenClaw and OpenAI Codex to support modern development workflows.
